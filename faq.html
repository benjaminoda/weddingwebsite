Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

  1. What is the date and time of the wedding?

    Answer: Our wedding will take place on May 10th, 2025. Please plan to arrive at the venue at 5 PM for the wedding welcome, as the ceremony will begin at 5:30 PM.

  2. Where is the wedding venue?

    Answer: The wedding ceremony and reception will be held at The Wall Street Hotel, located at 88 Wall Street, New York, NY 10005.

  3. Is there a dress code for the wedding?

    Answer: Yes, the dress code for our wedding is black-tie optional. Men: Preferably wear a tuxedo and bow tie, otherwise a dark suit and tie. Women: Please wear an evening gown, formal cocktail dress, or dressy separates.

Event Details

  1. What time should I arrive at the venue?

    Answer: We recommend arriving at least 30 minutes before the ceremony (5 PM) starts to allow time for check-in and seating.

  2. Are children allowed at the wedding?

    Answer: We love your little ones, but we respectfully request an adults-only celebration.

  3. What should I do if I have dietary restrictions?

    Answer: Please let us know of any dietary restrictions when you RSVP or directly and we will do our best to accommodate your needs.

  4. How do I RSVP?

    Answer: You can RSVP via the Greenvelope invitation sent to you via text or email. Please let us know if you need the invitation to be resent.

  5. Can I bring a +1 to the wedding?

    Answer: To keep our event intimate, we are unable to accommodate additional guests beyond those listed on your invitation. Please reach out to us directly if you have questions or concerns.

Travel and Accommodation

  1. Are there hotel room blocks for guests?

    Answer: Yes, we will have room blocks reserved at nearby hotels. We will update this information at least six months before the wedding date. Please check back for details and booking links.

  2. What are the recommended hotels to stay at?

    Answer: We recommend the following hotels: The Wall Street Hotel (venue), The Beekman, A Thompson Hotel, and World Center Hotel. Check our "Travel" page for more details and estimated prices.

Logistics and Miscellaneous

  1. What is the best way to get around the city?

    Answer: New York City has excellent public transportation, including subways and buses. Ride shares like Lyft, Uber, and Revel are also convenient options.

  2. Can I take photos during the ceremony?

    Answer: Yes! Please feel free to take non-flash photography during the ceremony. We have also hired a professional photographer to capture the moment.

  3. What will the weather be like?

    Answer: May in New York City typically features mild to warm weather, with average temperatures ranging from 55°F to 70°F. We recommend bringing a light jacket for cooler evenings.

  4. Who should I contact if I have any questions?

    Answer: You can reach out to us through our personal numbers or emails, otherwise, please leave us a note in the form at the bottom of the page.

  5. Is there a plan for inclement weather?

    Answer: The ceremony and reception will be held indoors at The Wall Street Hotel, so we are prepared for any weather conditions.

  6. What should I do if I need to cancel my RSVP?

    Answer: If you need to cancel your RSVP, please update your attendance status via the Greenvelope invitation sent to you via text or email.

  7. How do I get from the airport to the hotel?

    Answer: You can take a taxi, ride share (Lyft, Uber, or Revel), or public transportation (NJ Transit train + PATH train). Check our "Travel" page for detailed instructions.

  8. Is parking available at the venue?

    Answer: Limited valet parking is available at The Wall Street Hotel. Additional parking can be found at nearby garages.
